The companies with specific, measurable AI applications will get acquired at premium valuations. The generic "AI platforms" will struggle to differentiate.

Domain-wise, this means the specific application domains ("SalesAgent.com", "LegalAI.com", "CodeAgent.com") will appreciate faster than broad category domains ("AI.com", "ArtificialIntelligence.com").
